StreamFlow by TechNova — Product FAQ and Support Guide

Overview
StreamFlow is TechNova's real-time event-streaming platform. It ingests market and transaction events, applies windowed aggregations, and raises alerts when thresholds are breached. It is available in three tiers: Starter, Growth, and Enterprise.

Pricing and Plans
The Starter plan costs 4,900 rupees per month and supports up to 1 million events per day. The Growth plan costs 18,000 rupees per month and supports up to 20 million events per day. The Enterprise plan is custom-priced and includes a dedicated support engineer. All plans are billed monthly and can be cancelled at any time with 15 days' notice.

Refund Policy
New customers may request a full refund within 14 days of their first payment, no questions asked. After 14 days, refunds are issued on a pro-rata basis only if StreamFlow fails to meet the 99.9% uptime commitment described in the Service Level Agreement. Refunds are processed within 7 business days to the original payment method.

Data Retention
Event data is retained for 30 days on the Starter plan, 90 days on the Growth plan, and up to 365 days on the Enterprise plan. After the retention window, raw events are permanently deleted, but aggregated hourly summaries are kept for two years on all plans.

Uptime and SLA
StreamFlow guarantees 99.9% monthly uptime on the Growth and Enterprise plans. The Starter plan is offered on a best-effort basis with no formal uptime guarantee. If the Growth or Enterprise uptime falls below 99.9% in a calendar month, affected customers receive a 10% service credit for that month.

Support Channels
Starter customers receive email support with a 48-hour response target. Growth customers receive email and chat support with a 12-hour response target. Enterprise customers receive 24/7 phone support with a 1-hour response target for critical incidents.

Security
All data in StreamFlow is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256. StreamFlow is ISO 27001 certified and undergoes an independent penetration test every year in the month of August.
