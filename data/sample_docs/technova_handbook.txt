TechNova Solutions — Employee Handbook (2026 Edition)

1. About TechNova
TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 2019 and is headquartered in Pune, India. The company builds data-streaming and analytics products for the fintech sector. As of January 2026 it employs 480 people across three offices: Pune, Bengaluru, and a small research unit in Nashik.

2. Working Hours and Leave
Standard working hours are 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday, with a one-hour lunch break. Employees are entitled to 24 days of paid annual leave per calendar year, in addition to 12 public holidays. Unused annual leave of up to 8 days may be carried over into the next year; anything beyond that lapses on 31 March. Sick leave is capped at 10 days per year and requires a medical certificate for absences longer than two consecutive days.

3. Remote Work Policy
TechNova follows a hybrid model. Employees must work from an office at least three days per week. Fully remote arrangements require director-level approval and are reviewed every six months. The company reimburses up to 2,000 rupees per month towards home internet for approved remote staff.

4. Expense Reimbursement
Business expenses must be submitted through the Concur portal within 30 days of being incurred. Claims submitted after 30 days are rejected automatically. Meal expenses during client visits are reimbursed up to 1,200 rupees per day. All claims above 10,000 rupees require prior written approval from a line manager.

5. Code of Conduct
All employees must complete the annual security-awareness training by 30 September each year. Failure to complete it results in temporary suspension of VPN access until the training is finished. Sharing customer data outside approved systems is grounds for immediate termination.

6. Performance Reviews
Performance reviews happen twice a year, in June and December. Ratings run on a five-point scale from 1 (Needs Improvement) to 5 (Outstanding). Promotions are considered only during the December cycle. An employee must have a minimum tenure of nine months to be eligible for a promotion.
